Pacific Crest Trail facts

  • 2663 miles
  • Elevation: sea level - 13,153ft
  • 25 National Forests
  • 7 National Parks
  • Mexico to Canada through 3 States:
    • California
    • Oregon
    • Washington
  • Roughly 180 thru-hikers complete the trail each year
