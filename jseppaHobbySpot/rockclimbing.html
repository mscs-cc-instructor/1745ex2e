Rock climbing is the sport of climbing rock faces. (pretty self-explanatory). There are two areas of rock climbing: bouldering and walls.

Bouldering is climbing on short and technical 'boulders'. These are usually only 15-20ft tall and don't require any equipment other than shoes.

Wall climbing is climbing whole faces of rock. These can be anywhere from 30ft tall to 1000's of feet in the air.
